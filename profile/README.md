## Remix

**Remix** is being developed for Stoat as the first open-source music bot. It was founded in 2019 by [@NoLogicAlan](https://github.com/NoLogicAlan) and [@fanticwastaken](https://github.com/fanticwastaken) and brought to Stoat by [@ShadowLp174](https://github.com/ShadowLp174) and which was once known as your music bot on Discord. Links to important project resources are provided below.
